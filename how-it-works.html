🎯 What is BTTS?

Both Teams To Score (BTTS) is a popular football betting market where you predict whether both teams will score at least one goal during the match.

  • BTTS Yes — Both teams score (e.g., 1-1, 2-1, 3-2)
  • BTTS No — At least one team fails to score (e.g., 1-0, 0-0, 3-0)

This market is popular because it doesn't require you to predict the winner — just whether both teams will find the net.

📊 Why xG (Expected Goals)?

Expected Goals (xG) measures the quality of scoring chances, not just actual goals scored.

The Problem with Actual Goals

A team that scores 0 goals from 2.5 xG worth of chances isn't bad — they're unlucky. Next game, they'll likely score. Actual goals have high variance due to finishing quality, goalkeeper performance, and random bounces.

Why xG is Better

  • Removes luck — Measures shot quality, not outcomes
  • More predictive — xG correlates better with future scoring than past goals
  • Industry standard — Used by professional clubs, betting syndicates, and analysts

💡 Example: If Leeds has xG of 1.78 per game but only scores 1.5, they're underperforming their chances. The model uses 1.78, expecting regression to mean.

🔢 The Poisson Calculation

The Poisson distribution models the probability of a given number of goals, based on the expected average (xG).

P(X = k) = (λ^k × e^(-λ)) / k! Where λ = Expected Goals (xG)

For BTTS, we calculate:

  • P(Home scores 0) = P(X=0) with λ = Home xG
  • P(Away scores 0) = P(X=0) with λ = Away xG
  • P(BTTS Yes) = (1 - P(Home=0)) × (1 - P(Away=0))

Example: Leeds (1.78 xG) vs Sheffield Utd (1.50 xG)

P(Leeds scores 0) = e^(-1.78) = 16.9% P(Sheffield scores 0) = e^(-1.50) = 22.3% P(BTTS Yes) = (1 - 0.169) × (1 - 0.223) = 0.831 × 0.777 = 64.6%

💎 Value Bet Detection

A "value bet" occurs when our calculated probability is higher than the bookmaker's implied probability.

Implied Probability = 1 / Decimal Odds Value Rating = Our Probability - Implied Probability Example: Our prob = 65%, Odds = 1.80 (implied 55.6%) Value = 65% - 55.6% = +9.4% edge
